Compete in White Collar Boxing

Get Yourself Fight Ready

With one-on-one coaching you will quickly master the basics, and be ready to take things further. Depending on your goals, you will be able to compete in our famous White Collar Boxing Nights.

White Collar Boxing Night is a special boxing event for people who have not fought before, and who usually come from white collar professions, like business or law.

Almost everyone can compete in White Collar Boxing Night, no matter their age, experience, or fitness level. What matters most is willingness and determination.

If you are interested, we will train you for several months in advance, and have you fight ready. The event itself is very safe - the challenge is in the preparation and commitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should i bring equipemnt?

Equipment like gloves and head protection etc is present at the gym but it would be recomended to buy your own wich you can do at the gym itself

What if I've never boxed before?

You can start learning boxing with us even if you have zero experience with the sport. We will take care of you and show you how to start training in a safe way. Many of our pupils made great progress in a short time.

Should I do private or group coaching?

For anyone who is serious about progressing with boxing, we recommend you try individual one-on-one coaching first, and see if it is right for you. Since we are a dedicated boxing gym, we love to work with those who are really focused on learning the best technique, and seeing the best results. For these types of people one-on-one coaching is perfect.

If, on the other hand, you are looking for more of a social way to get fit, and just learn the basics of boxing, then maybe group classes are a better option. Since group classes are more focused on overall techniques in a group setting, they are more budget friendly and social.
